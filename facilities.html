Boys Hostel


YMCA Faridabad has a boys hostel for providing accommodation purposes. Each boys hostel has one reading room, common room, table tennis room, etc. All the hostels have wifi facilities to provide students with an internet connection. Each hostel has a separate warden for proper administration and discipline. YMCA Faridabad boys facilities are well equipped to fulfill the basic needs of the students.
